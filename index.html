How we choose our picks

We don’t run timed trials or lab tests. Instead, we read deeply: thousands of owner reviews across multiple retailers, discussions on whiskey forums (Reddit, Straight Bourbon, Scotchit), and the verdicts of respected journalists at sites like Whisky Advocate and The Whiskey Wash.
